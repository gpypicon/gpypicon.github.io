Profile

Guillaume Picon is a Generalist Engineer and Specialist in Global Information Technology, International Projects and Internet.

He is based in Paris, France.

Born in Lyon, France in 1979, he followed studies in Sciences and Technology, and has a career of more than 20 years in the sector of Information Technology. Travelling around the world, living in Ireland during 3 years and in Switzerland for 10 years, he speaks or understands currently 5 languages : French, English, German/Swiss German, Portuguese, Spanish by chance encountering different Cultures and Horizons.

He is a Senior Scientific Engineer, IEEE member, and finally an Expert System Engineer, specialized in the domains of General Engineering, Technology, Complex Systems and Knowledge Management (KM) as a professional practitioner and punctual Researcher.

Graduated from different schools, universities (ECAM Lyon - Master in Sciences "Arts & Metiers", MIT OpenCourseWare, and Advanced Studies with Post-Docs) in major disciplines of Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Industrial Management. He worked as IT professional for companies like IBM, HP, ATOS, XEROX, ROCHE in France, Ireland, Switzerland, in relation with many regions of the World, before launching a Startup Company called INFORGETIX.

Inforgetix is a pioneer in Web-Assisted Quantum Complexity Machines, aiming to provide essentially "Tools for Tomorrow", especially in the context of General and Special Complexity Chronodynamics, such as representing highly-variated social and bio-psychic phenomenons, cultural, socio-technic, industrial or technologic systems, structures and processes with dynamically organized knowledge trees, second-generation quantum models generators (QM2), and Complex Systems Theory Computational Assistance, such as innovative Relativized System Engineering (RSE) technics to provide capable Information Systems in Chronography, Operational Safety, Prevision, Strategy & Anticipation. This company also provides real project support for all kinds of interesting ideas, covering more traditional technology, such as Cloud, CI-CD projects, System Engineering, System Integration, System Administration and Web Development.
